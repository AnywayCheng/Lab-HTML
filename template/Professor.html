Research Experience

Development of a comprehensive pipeline and tool for identifying neoantigens in cancers.(2021 – Present)

  • Proposing a method and analytic pipeline to identify neoantigens from DNA-seq, RNA-seq, and/or liquid chromatography-tandem mass spectrometry data.
  • Establishing an online database of tumor neoantigens in the Taiwanese population.

Development of a deep learning-based method for predicting microRNA targets and an integrated database.(2021 – Present)

  • Development of a novel algorithm for predicting targets by employing the U-Net architecture based on a convolutional neural network.
  • Integrating the proposed algorithm with other existing ones as an online platform for providing a reliable predicted result.

Microbial genome or metagenomics studies.(2021 – Present)

  • Applying a machine learning-based imputation method to improve the current limitations of the Mycobacterium tuberculosis genomic data on transmission inference.
  • Prediction of drug resistance genes in non-tuberculosis mycobacterium from whole-genome sequencing data using a genetic algorithm.
  • Exploring the associations between gut microbiota and the pediatric diseases of infants in Taiwan.
  • Using metagenomic approaches and linear discriminant analysis to identify gut microbiota associated with metabolic syndrome.

A scalable high-throughput pipeline for inferring the molecular mechanisms of non-coding genetic variants.(2019 – 2021)

  • Development of a post-GWAS tool with scalable, extensible, and distributed computing architecture based on Apache Spark.
  • Integration of large-scale functional genomics data for annotation of genetic variants.
  • Inferring the molecular mechanisms via tissue contexts, target genes, and downstream biological processes underlying GWAS findings.

De novo genome assembly and phylogenetic analyses of Mikado and Swinhoe’s pheasants (Syrmaticus mikado and Lophura swinhoii) indigenous to Taiwan.(2014 – 2018)

  • Genome assembly and annotation of Mikado and Swinhoe’s pheasants.
  • Identification of gene families and phylogenetic analyses.
  • Examination of genes under positive selection and enrichment analysis.
  • Identification of the MHC-B region of the Mikado pheasant.
  • Investigation of the evolutionary history among long-tailed pheasants.

Development of web-based online systems for high-throughput data analyses and visualization.(2010 – 2021)

  • MiDSystem: A comprehensive online system for bacterial de novo genome assembly.
  • VariED: The first integrated database of gene annotation and expression profiles for variants related to human diseases.
  • anamiR: Integrated analysis of microRNA and gene expression profiling.
  • CellExpress: A comprehensive microarray-based cancer cell line and clinical sample gene expression analysis online system.
  • Development of the i5k Workspace for annotation and visualization of insect genomes.
  • miRSystem: An integrated system for characterizing enriched functions and pathways of microRNA targets.

Propose a prediction algorithm for microRNA targets using a Hidden Markov Model.(2007 – 2009)

Development of Cancer Repository System, a web-based management system for digitalization and consolidation of clinical records and specimen data from cancer patients.(2007 – 2008)
